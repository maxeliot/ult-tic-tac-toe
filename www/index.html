Ultimate-Tic-Tac-Toe Rules (show/hide)

It's like a normal tic-tac-toe, except each cell is composed of an inner tic-tac-toe.
You have to win 3 sub-tic-tac-toe's in a row, column or diagonal.
Your move sends the opponent to the bigger cell of the subcell you clicked, where they have to play.
You will understand by playing, Good luck!
